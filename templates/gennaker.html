ORC Spinnaker Area Calculator

This calculator estimates the area of a spinnaker or gennaker based on the ORC measurement rule:

Area = ASL × (SFL + 4 × SHW) ÷ 6
where:
- ASL = (SLU + SLE) ÷ 2
- SLU = length of luff (front edge)
- SLE = length of leech (back edge)
- SHW = sail half-width, measured at 50% height
- SFL = foot length (bottom of sail)
